The Cult Behind the Craft

Ill-Fated Idols is a small studio conjuring dark, eldritch, and Lovecraftian sculptures — each one 3D-printed, then hand-painted into being.

Every idol begins as a digital sculpt, is summoned layer by layer on the print bed, and is finished by hand: primed, shaded, and detailed until the thing on your shelf looks like it remembers a world older than ours. No two are exactly alike.

Whether you collect cosmic horror, run a tabletop campaign, or simply want something unsettling watching over your desk — there's an idol here waiting to be claimed.

  • 3D-printed in durable resin & PLA
  • Hand-painted, one at a time
  • Original eldritch designs
  • Made to order — yours alone
